Doja Cat

American rapper and singer

 
    Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini 
    (Zulu pronunciation: ['zandile 'É®amini];
     born October 21, 1995), known professionally as Doja Cat,
    is an American rapper and singer. Born and raised in Los Angeles, 
    she began making and releasing music on SoundCloud as a teenager. 
    Her song "So High" caught the attention of Kemosabe and RCA Records, 
    with whom she signed a recording contract prior to the release 
    of her debut extended play, Purrr! in 2014.
